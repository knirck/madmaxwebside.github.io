  • Founding of MADMAX (MAgnetized Disc and Mirror Axion eXperiment)-Collaboration

    On the 18th of October 2017 the MADMAX-Collaboration was founded. Founding Insitutes are the Max-Planck-Institut für Physik in Muncih, the Universities of Aachen, Hamburg, and Tübingen in Germany the University of Zaragoza in Spanien, the CEA-IRFU in Saclay, France, and the Deutschen-Elektronen-Synchrotrons (DESY) in Hamburg.
